Review the current codebase and aggressively challenge every design decision. For each class, function, and module:

1. Question whether the chosen approach is the best one — propose at least one alternative
2. Identify hidden assumptions that could break under different conditions
3. Find edge cases that are not handled
4. Challenge naming choices — are they precise and self-documenting?
5. Look for violations of SOLID principles, even subtle ones
6. Identify any unnecessary complexity — could this be simpler?
7. Check for missing abstractions or leaky abstractions
8. Question whether the error handling covers all failure modes
9. Assess testability — is every component easy to test in isolation?
10. Look for implicit coupling between components

Be direct and critical. Do not soften feedback. Present findings ranked by severity.
